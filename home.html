Brassica Oleracea: The cultivars

Brassica oleracea is a plant species that includes many common foods as cultivars, including
cabbage, broccoli, cauliflower, kale, Brussels sprouts, collard greens, Savoy cabbage, kohlrabi, and gai lan.
In its uncultivated form, it is called wild cabbage, and is native to coastal southern and western Europe.
